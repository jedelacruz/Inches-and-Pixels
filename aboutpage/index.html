About —

Welcome to Inches and Pixels, a cutting-edge application developed using VB.NET and Microsoft SQL Access. Designed specifically for printing services, Inches and Pixels provides a streamlined and efficient order system tailored to your unique needs. Whether you're a student, faculty member, or staff at Lyceum of Alabang, our application is here to simplify your printing requirements.


With Inches and Pixels, you can easily place print orders for various materials, including documents, flyers, posters, and more. Our user-friendly interface allows you to specify the exact dimensions in both inches and pixels, ensuring precise and accurate printing every time. Say goodbye to guesswork and hello to professional-quality prints that perfectly match your vision.


As a thesis project developed by our dedicated team, Inches and Pixels is the result of extensive research and meticulous attention to detail. We understand the importance of a reliable and efficient printing system, especially in a busy educational environment. Experience the convenience of Inches and Pixels and witness how it revolutionizes the way you handle printing services at Lyceum of Alabang.
